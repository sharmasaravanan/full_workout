07
Rest Day
Full Rest
No Lifting No HIIT Recover
😴
Prioritize Sleep
7–9 hours. Muscle grows during recovery, not during training.
💧
Stay Hydrated
Aim for 2.5–3L of water. Replenish electrolytes if needed.
🥗
Nail Your Nutrition
Hit protein goals (0.8–1g/lb bodyweight). Focus on whole foods and recovery meals.
🤸
Light Mobility (Optional)
10–15 min gentle mobility work only if you feel the urge. No intensity.
Progression Rule
If you hit all reps comfortably last week → increase weight or add 1–2 reps next week. Small consistent gains compound over time.
